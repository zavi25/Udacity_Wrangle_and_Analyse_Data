Action Report¶

Cleaned Dataset Description¶

After cleaning the datasets and merging into one dataset, the final dataset consists of 1881 rows and 23 columns, the columns include:

  • tweet_id - Unique id for each tweet.
  • source - The device the tweet originated from.
  • text - The text contained in the tweet.
  • expanded_urls - The tweet Url's.
  • rating_numerator - The rating numerator out of 10.
  • rating_denominator - The rating denominator taken as 10.
  • name - The dog name.
  • dog_size - The classification of the dogs by size
  • date - The date the tweet was made.
  • time - The time of the tweet
  • jpg_url - The dog image url
  • img_num - The image number
  • p1 - The algorithm's #1 prediction
  • p1_conf - Accuracy confindence for the #1 prediction
  • p1_dog - Whether or not the #1 prediction is a breed of dog
  • p2 - The algorithm's #2 prediction
  • p2_conf -Accuracy confindence for the #2 prediction
  • p2_dog - Whether or not the #2 prediction is a breed of dog
  • p3 - The algorithm's #3 prediction
  • p3_conf - Accuracy confindence for the #3 prediction
  • p3_dog - Whether or not the #3 prediction is a breed of dog
  • retweet_count - The number of retweets per tweet
  • favorite_count - The number of likes per tweet
